Mac and Cheese

Description

Mac and cheese is one of my favourite foods. it is a simple recipe and a favourite for kids everywhere. It is a dish consisting of macaroni pasta and cheese sauce.

Ingredients

  • macaroni
  • cheese
  • butter
  • milk
  • flour
  • seasoning

Steps

  1. Boil the macaroni in salted water until the noodles are al dente. Drain and transfer to a dish.
  2. Melt butter, then whisk in the flour. Whisk in the milk, bring to a simmer, and stir in the cheeses. Season with salt and pepper and continue simmering until the sauce is thick. Pour the sauce over the noodles and stir.

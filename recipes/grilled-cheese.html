Grilled Cheese

Description

Grilled cheese is one of my favourite foods. The recipe is simple but it is still delicious. It is a hot sandwich made with buttered and toasted bread andfilled with American cheese.

Ingredients

  • bread
  • cheese
  • butter

Steps

  1. Butter one side of the bread and place it butter side down on the stove.
  2. Put the cheese on top of the bread and anothet another piece of bread, buttered side up, on top of that.
  3. Wait till the bread on bottom is toasted and then flip it. When that side is toasted then move to a dish and youre' done.
